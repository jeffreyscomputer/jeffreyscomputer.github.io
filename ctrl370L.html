Vibrating String Tuning Control System: The "Unolin"

I made this single string instrument as the final project for a digital control laboratory class. The whole course was an exercise in controling the temperature inside a test tube by varying the current through a power resistor using a digital controller based around the BS2 MCU, programmed in PBASIC. Several types of controllers were made - first a nonlinear on/off controller, then a variation with a tolerance band and a bias control signal, proportional control, PI control, PD control, and then a PID controller.

Since the device I made represented a stable plant with a transfer function well approximated by a simple integrator, I could have made a simple on/off controller and had good results. However, I wanted to see if I could just tune the PID controller I made for the temperature control problem to work with this new plant. And it worked! But it is really slow, and not musical. Enjoy on youtube.
